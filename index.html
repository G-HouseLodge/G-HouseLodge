ABOUT US

Check In

There is flexible Check in time for all clients. Guest are kindly requested to complete the check in proceedure at the reception before entering the room.

check Out

Check-out before 12:00 PM, Late check-out subject to availability, A late check-out fee may be charged.

Promotios

We regularly offer attractive deals to our customers. Please ask at reception for any current deals for your big event or stay.

Reception

Our receptionists give a warm welcome to our guests. They are responsible for verifying their identity, and providing them with room keys and other helpful information.

Security Precaution

Guests are to note that the House is not liable for loss of customer’s valuables. Guests are responsible for the safe keeping of all their valuables during their stay with G-House.

Resturant

G-House provides bar services, drinks and meals. We actively encourage our guests to try our specially sourced food and drinks.

Payment

Guests are to settle their bills before their stay or ahead of each night stay with us unless a standing or prior arrangement is made with management.

Minors

Minors up to 16 years are not allowed in the bar on the House premises unless accompanied by an adult.
